INDIVIDUAL CONTRIBUTOR AGREEMENT FOR OpenBACH
(“Contributor Agreement”)

Thank you for your participation in the OpenBACH Open Source Community (“OpenBACH Community”), an open source community originally created and sponsored by the “Centre National des Etudes Spatiales” (“CNES”) (the “CNES” being designed in this agreement as the “Steering Committee”).

“OpenBACH” is the name of the software engineering and research platform, including its documentation, the aim of which is to provide a benchmark of tests supervision/monitoring. It has been promoted afterwards by CNES as a reference open-source software tool for use within its research and development studies and activities in the domain of satellite communication systems and networks.

By distributing and making available the source codes of OpenBACH components “as is” under the terms and conditions of the GNU General Public License version 3 (“GPL”), the intention of the CNES is to facilitate its use and encourage any Contributions, improvement and perpetuation of OpenBACH for the benefit of the whole OpenBACH Community, the objective of the CNES being also to optimize and rationalize more the research and development in this domain, avoid duplication of efforts and therefore facilitate programs of new satellite solutions. In order to better exchange, share and learn information about OpenBACH, contributors will have also the possibility to participate to meetings organized and announced by the Steering Committee of OpenBACH Community on the web site http://www.openbach.org.

In this Contributor Agreement, “Contribution” shall mean any and all original work(s) of authorship, including any modifications, corrections, translations, adaptations, additions and/or new functions integrated into OpenBACH as well as any or all module, that is (intentionally) submitted by any copyright owner (whether individual or company) (« Contributor(s) »), by any electronic or written means, to the Steering Committee of OpenBACH Community, for possible inclusion in OpenBACH. (Nothing in this Agreement shall be deemed to require the Steering Committee of OpenBACH Community to include the Contribution in any OpenBACH version).
